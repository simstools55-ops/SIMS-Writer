# Quality Specification v0.2.1 RC

## Frozen additions

- Diagnosis is an array; `LOW_SAMPLE` may coexist with another diagnosis.
- `INTENT_MISMATCH` is a first-class diagnosis.
- Strong bodies under `POSITION_OPPORTUNITY` receive SERP-first local edits.
- Preservation Score records how much valuable existing content remains protected.
- Change Budget is the estimated percentage of article components materially changed; it is a guardrail, not fabricated precision.
- Long Before/After content is displayed inside a fixed-height vertical-scroll frame.
- SIMS_FEEDBACK_V2 retains V1 operational fields and adds diagnosis, preservation, rewrite, risk, and validation fields.
